Fairloy Card provides the best and easiest way for the customers to see how much they saved on their purchases at our participating clients. Once you Sign Up on our portal you will see the dollars you saved on your previous purchase.

How it works

___ _ __

Fairloy is one card which can be used at all participating client locations not just in one business location
The amount collected doesn't have a conversion rate, it represents the exact dollar amount available to the customer for use
Customers will see the exact dollar amount they save and can redeem at very next transaction at the same client location
Advanced dashboard with many features such as number of returning customers, in App information provides feedback directly to the business owner
Monthly customer Data Analytic Report (DAR) which will help you to analyze customer spending habits at your business
Our Loyalty card can be used as a Gift Card as well. Individuals also have the option to purchase gift cards for your location
